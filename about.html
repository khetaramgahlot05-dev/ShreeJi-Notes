About ShreeJi Notes

Our Story

ShreeJi Notes was founded in 2024 by Kheta Ram, a passionate B.Sc. B.Ed. student at Mohanlal Sukhadia University (MLSU), Rajasthan. During my first year of studies, I realized that while there were many study resources available, finding affordable, well-organized, and MLSU-specific notes was challenging. Most students struggled with scattered information and incomplete notes.

Our Mission

To empower students at MLSU and beyond by providing high-quality, affordable, and exam-focused study notes. We believe that quality education should be accessible to everyone, and our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional textbooks and the specific needs of modern learners.

Why ShreeJi Notes?

ShreeJi Notes stands out because every note is created by top-performing students who understand exactly what you need for your exams. Our notes are:

  • ✓ Strictly aligned with the MLSU syllabus
  • ✓ Unit-wise organized for easy revision
  • ✓ Focused on exam-relevant topics only
  • ✓ Created by students who scored 90%+
  • ✓ Updated regularly to match the latest syllabus
  • ✓ Available instantly after payment verification

Our Subjects

Currently, we offer comprehensive notes for:

  • Zoology (1st, 2nd, 3rd Year)
  • Botany (1st, 2nd, 3rd Year)
  • Chemistry (1st, 2nd, 3rd Year)

We're expanding to include more subjects and programs based on student feedback.

Our Commitment

We're committed to providing exceptional quality and customer service. Every note goes through a rigorous review process before being published. We also maintain a 100% refund policy for unsatisfied customers and provide 24/7 support through WhatsApp and email.

Contact Us

Have questions or suggestions? We'd love to hear from you! Visit our Contact Page or reach out on WhatsApp for quick responses.
