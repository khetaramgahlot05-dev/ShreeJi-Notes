Delivery Information

How PDF Notes are Delivered

ShreeJi Notes delivers all PDF study materials instantly after payment verification. Here's what you need to know:

Delivery Process

  1. You complete payment via UPI QR code
  2. Upload your payment screenshot in the checkout modal
  3. Our team verifies your payment (usually 4-6 hours)
  4. PDF download link is sent to your registered email
  5. You can download and access the PDF immediately on any device

Delivery Timeline

Standard Delivery: 4-6 hours from payment verification

Weekend/Holiday: May take up to 24 hours

Instant Access: Download links are valid for lifetime access

What You Receive

  • High-quality PDF notes in A4 format
  • Unit-wise organized content as per MLSU syllabus
  • Diagrams, tables, and important highlights
  • Lifetime access to your purchased PDF

Device Compatibility

Our PDF notes work on:

  • ✓ Windows PC and Mac
  • ✓ Android phones and tablets
  • ✓ iPhone and iPad
  • ✓ Any device with a PDF reader

Access Your PDF

Email Link: You'll receive a download link via email. Simply click to download.

Direct Download: Click the link from your email or order confirmation page.

Store Locally: Save the PDF on your device for offline access anytime.

Troubleshooting

Can't find the email? Check your spam/junk folder. If still not found, contact us via WhatsApp.

Download link expired? Contact support and we'll resend it immediately.

PDF won't open? Install a PDF reader app (Adobe Reader, built-in readers, etc.) and try again.

Contact Support

If you face any delivery issues, reach out to us immediately:

  • WhatsApp: Quick response for urgent issues
  • Email: support@shreejinotes.com
  • Contact Form: Available 24/7
