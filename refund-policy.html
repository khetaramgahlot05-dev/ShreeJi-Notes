Refund Policy

100% Satisfaction Guarantee

At ShreeJi Notes, we stand behind the quality of our study materials. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a hassle-free refund policy.

Refund Eligibility

You're eligible for a full refund if:

  • You request a refund within 7 days of purchase
  • You have not shared or distributed the PDF to others
  • You provide valid reasons for dissatisfaction
  • The PDF has been properly delivered to your email

Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will NOT be provided in the following cases:

  • Refund request after 7 days of purchase
  • PDF has been shared with others
  • Fraudulent or duplicate payments
  • Incorrect UPI ID or payment details
  • Customer technical issues (device, internet, etc.)

How to Request a Refund

Step 1: Contact us via WhatsApp or email with your order details

Step 2: Provide your order ID and reason for refund request

Step 3: Our team reviews your request within 24-48 hours

Step 4: Upon approval, refund is processed within 5-7 business days

Refund Processing Time

After approval, refunds are processed through the same UPI gateway used for payment. You should see the amount credited back to your account within 5-7 business days, depending on your bank's processing time.

Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be considered in cases where:

  • Content is partially used or accessed
  • Minor technical issues with the PDF
  • Incomplete syllabus coverage (at our discretion)

Defective or Missing Content

If you receive a defective PDF or the content is missing/incomplete:

  1. Contact us immediately with proof (screenshots)
  2. We'll provide a replacement PDF for free
  3. If replacement is unsatisfactory, full refund will be processed

Contact for Refund Requests

Email: support@shreejinotes.com

WhatsApp: +91 9999 999 999

Response Time: Within 24-48 hours

Cancellation of Orders

If you wish to cancel an order before payment verification, please contact us immediately. Cancellations are processed within 24-48 hours and full amount is refunded.

Important Notes

  • Each refund request is evaluated individually
  • We reserve the right to refuse refunds that violate this policy
  • Repeated refund requests may result in account suspension
  • By accepting terms, you agree not to share the PDF after refund
